Abendrot by Hakanai (2020)

“Abendrot” is a song I wrote in 2016 that did not progress past the initial stages until I brought it to my drummer friend Peter in early 2020. He convinced me to record and release it, which we did together in the early stages of the global pandemic. The result is a deep and textural indie rock song about the misty synthesis of love and its dissolution.
For this song, I recorded and engineered all the parts; played guitar, bass, synth, and piano; wrote the lyrics and sang; mixed and mastered the track; and did the cover design and photography. It acts as a single preceding a full-length album to be released later in 2021
