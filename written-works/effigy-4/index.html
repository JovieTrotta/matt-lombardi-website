Effigy #4, Day 2:

Shinjuku Gyo-en National Garden, Shinjuku Ward, Tokyo

迷子になった
(I got lost.)
the songs of the cicadas,
bury their way into the cochlea.
a serene scene of sunshine untattered,
reverential moss and idle minds scattered,
floating on ponds of reticence.
I will cross an heirloom footbridge,
creaking anxiously all the while,
only to find myself back where I began,
lost among the viridian reeds,
where serrated grasses lash at my arm.
orb-weavers brace against dusty
corners,
laying siege against a sweetly sallow pagoda.
an ancient rite, in sleepless contemplation.
calm the nerves and stilted air,
by the heavenly altars of shade and
shame.
